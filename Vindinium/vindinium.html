Vindinium & AI

What is AI?

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. The Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines AI as "the capability of a machine to imitate intelligent human behavior". In other words, this term can be used to describe computers capable of displaying similar thinking processes to humans.

What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is the name of an artificial intelligence game in which players can control their bot through orders given in the form of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. The object of the game is to have your bot collect the highest amount of gold by stealing gold mines. In order to claim such gold mines, your bot must successfully defeat the goblins that guard it. By fighting with these goblins, as well as other bots for ownership of such gold mines, you may lose some of your health. In order to become healthy again, it is very important to locate and travel to taverns where recovery can take place.
